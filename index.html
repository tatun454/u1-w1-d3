Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. it will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN: Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a parin knife is desired, though it'not necessary)

Method

1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though i sometimes knead it few times i the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
